Popular FAQ's

What kind of gasoline should I utilize in my Mercedes-Benz?

Mercedes-Benz gasoline engines require the use of premium unleaded gasoline (91 octane or higher).

There is an "Anti-theft protection active" message on my COMAND screen

There is an "Anti-theft protection active" message on my COMAND screen When a COMAND unit is installed into a vehicle (whether during production or as a replacement) it is equipped with a PIN. This PIN will be associated to the VIN and relies on the communication network within the vehicle for validation. The network check from COMAND is performed at startup and i f the internal vehicle network is not detected, "Anti-Theft protection active" will be displayed.

To find out whether your vehicle is involved in a recall or other campaigns, please click here to enter your VIN (Vehicle Identification Number).If your vehicle is involved in a recall, please contact your preferred authorized Mercedes-Benz dealer to schedule an appointment at your earliest convenience.

Can I purchase extended warranty from Mercedes-Benz?

The Mercedes-Benz Extended Limited Warranty may be purchased through any authorized Mercedes-Benz dealership (restrictions and limitations apply). A surcharge will be added to the Manufacturer's Suggested Retail Price if purchased after 30 days from the new-vehicle warranty start date. Please contact your dealership for pricing and details, or click the following link for more information: Mercedes-Benz Warranty Information

How do I adjust the volume of the navigation voice?

Volume adjusting is only possible when the navigation announcements are in progress. You can repeat the previous announcement or wait for the next announcement to begin. When the voice is speaking, adjust the volume using the same method to lower/ raise the volume of your radio.
